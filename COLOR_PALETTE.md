# Brand Color Palette - Kimani Wambua & Company Advocates

## Primary Color Palette

Based on the PDF document analysis and legal branding best practices:

### Primary Colors

**Deep Navy - #1a2332**
- **Usage**: Primary brand color, headers, navigation, text
- **Psychology**: Trust, authority, professionalism, stability
- **Applications**: Backgrounds, main headings, CTAs

**Gold - #c9a961**
- **Usage**: Secondary/accent color, highlights, premium elements
- **Psychology**: Excellence, prestige, established, wisdom
- **Applications**: Accents, borders, highlights, special elements

### Supporting Colors

**Professional Blue - #2c5f8d**
- **Usage**: Links, secondary actions, hover states
- **Psychology**: Action, trust, reliability
- **Applications**: Interactive elements, links

**Light Surface - #f9fafb**
- **Usage**: Backgrounds, alternating sections
- **Psychology**: Clean, spacious, modern
- **Applications**: Section backgrounds, cards

**Neutral Gray - #6b7280**
- **Usage**: Body text, secondary information
- **Psychology**: Professional, readable
- **Applications**: Body copy, labels

**Border Gray - #e5e7eb**
- **Usage**: Dividers, borders, subtle separations
- **Psychology**: Subtle, unobtrusive
- **Applications**: Card borders, dividers

## Usage Guidelines

### Do's
✅ Use navy (#1a2332) as the primary color for headers and main elements
✅ Use gold (#c9a961) sparingly for highlights and premium features
✅ Maintain high contrast ratios for accessibility (WCAG AA: 4.5:1 minimum)
✅ Use neutral grays for body text to ensure readability
✅ Apply gold accents to draw attention to CTAs and important elements

### Don'ts
❌ Don't use bright, flashy colors that detract from professionalism
❌ Don't use gold as a background color (poor readability)
❌ Don't mix warm and cool tones excessively
❌ Don't use low-contrast combinations

## Accessibility

All color combinations meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards:
- Navy (#1a2332) on White (#ffffff): 14.8:1 ✅
- Gold (#c9a961) on Navy (#1a2332): 4.9:1 ✅
- Dark Text (#1f2937) on White (#ffffff): 16.1:1 ✅
- Gray Text (#6b7280) on White (#ffffff): 4.6:1 ✅

**Gold**: Represents excellence, prestige, and established reputation
**Blue**: Professional, reliable, action-oriented
**Gray**: Neutral, sophisticated, professional
