We oﬀer specialized legal services in property and conveyancing law. Our 
wide range of services include due-diligence searches on land and properties, 
drafting of sale agreements, transfers, charges and mortgages, residential 
and commercial leases, long term leases and transfer of lease, transfers of 
sectional titles and transfer by way of charge. We also handle subdivision 
of land, change of user, discharge of charges, caveats, cautions, easements, 
extension of expired government leases, licenses, powers of attorney, 
prohibitions, rectiﬁcation of titles, easements, sale of land, trusts relating to 
land, wills, applying for and obtaining the various consents required under the 
law, paying land rent and rates on behalf of clients, attendant stamping and 
registration formalities, and advising clients generally on all aspects of land 
law. We have particularly handled large scale real estate projects involving 
subdivision and resale of huge tracts of land.
We oﬀer specialized legal services to commercial banks and ﬁnancial 
institutions in relation to preparation and review of various security 
documents including charges and mortgages, corporate bonds and 
debentures, chattels mortgages, letters of hypothecation, deeds of guarantees 
and indemnity, simple loan agreement, syndicated loan agreements, and 
preparation of necessary board resolutions for companies and related 
stamping and registration formalities. We also routinely conduct searches on 
behalf of ﬁnancial institutions at the companies and business name registries, 
Registrar of Motor Vehicles, Registrar of Societies and self-help groups, and 
the NGO Coordination Board. Our Turn Around Time for conducting searches 
at the companies and business name registries is 24 hours.
We oﬀer specialized legal services on incorporation of businesses and other 
forms of associations including limited liability companies and companies lim-
ited by guarantee, trusts, non-governmental organizations (NGOs), Co-oper -
ative Societies, Societies and self-help groups, investment groups (“chamas”).  
We also routinely oﬀer advisory services on compliance with the various 
regulatory bodies and conversion of businesses from one mode to another, 
cessation and winding up of businesses or associations.OUR PRACTICE AREAS
